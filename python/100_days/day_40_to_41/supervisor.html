John Samuel - Broiler Breeder Farm Supervisor

Job Description

My main duties include overseeing the breeding, feeding, and general management of the poultry birds to ensure increased efficiency in production and the delivery of high-quality poultry products.

  • Maintained accurate records of crop yields, expenses, and labor costs, allowing for informed decision-making and cost reductions of 10%.

To address this, I collaborated with veterinary experts to implement preventive health measures and early disease detection protocols, leading to a significant improvement in bird health and overall farm performance.
